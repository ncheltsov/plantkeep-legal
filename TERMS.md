## 1. What Plantkeep is

Plantkeep is a personal plant-library Android app. It uses an AI service (Google Gemini, via Firebase AI Logic) to identify plants from photos you take, and stores your plant library locally on your device. There is no backend server, no user account, and no remote library hosting. See `PRIVACY.md` for details on data handling.
