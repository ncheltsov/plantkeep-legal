# Plantkeep — Data Deletion Request

**Last updated:** 2026-05-07

Plantkeep is a local-first plant library app. The library you build — including the photos you take — is stored only on your phone. Some diagnostic data is sent to Google services on our behalf (see the [privacy policy](./PRIVACY.md)).

## What gets deleted automatically

**Uninstall the app.** When you uninstall Plantkeep from your phone:

- Your saved plants and photos are deleted (they were only on your device).
- Your trial counter and unlock entitlement are deleted.
- Nothing is left on your phone.

## What requires a request

The following data lives on Google's servers, not on your phone, and is deleted on request:

- **Firebase Analytics events** — anonymous usage events (screen views, app version, etc.) keyed to a Firebase Installation ID.
- **Firebase Crashlytics reports** — crash stack traces, device model, OS version (only generated if the app crashed on your device).

## How to request deletion

Email **begimot@gmail.com** with the subject line **"Plantkeep — delete my data"**.

We will:

1. Acknowledge your request within 7 days.
2. Submit a deletion request to Google's Firebase services on your behalf.
3. Confirm completion in writing within 30 days (the GDPR statutory timeframe; shorter timelines apply under some other privacy laws).

You do not need to identify yourself — we have no account system, so there's nothing to authenticate. Mention any device or rough timeframe that helps us scope the deletion.
